Exhibit #3: Ron Kovic – A Vietnam Veteran Activist

Ron Kovic was a Vietnam Veteran who was paralyzed from the waist down. Coming back from Vietnam, he felt that he was done an injustice and fought back against the war. From his wheelchair he attended protests, took part in rallies, and even wrote a ground-breaking novel titled Born on the Fourth of July in 1976. This book, an ironic reference to the song “The Yankee Doodle Boy,” was made into a film (starring Tom Cruise!) and won many awards.
