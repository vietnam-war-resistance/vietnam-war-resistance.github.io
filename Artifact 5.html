Exhibit #5: Vietnam Veterans Protesting

This photo shows the organization Vietnam Veterans Against the War (VVAW) protesting in 1970. This group was formed in 1967 in New York City by six veterans who were tired of the war. VVAW gives GI’s the opportunity to speak their thoughts and “give voice to the growing opposition to the escalating war in Vietnam among people in the military.” They revealed information that was hidden from the public about the harsh treatment of the Vietnamese people. VVAW was integral in the anti-Vietnam war movement.
