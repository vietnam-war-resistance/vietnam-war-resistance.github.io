Exhibit #11: Protest Song “Bring ‘Em Home” by Pete Seeger

This song, released in 1963, was one of many protest songs made during the Vietnam War. Songs are a very effective way of protesting as they can spread easily and can be heard by many people around the globe. If it becomes a hit it can get attention from higher ups and perhaps cause some change. This song had lyrics that were against the war like “bring ‘em home” (referring to the soldiers in Vietnam) and "[these generals] don't have the right weaponry." In fact, Pete Seeger performed this song on The Johnny Cash Show in 1970. Although that may not seem so exceptional, the fact that he sang this extremely controversial song on national television is notable.
